ATOMS AND MOLECULES


➤ Atoms:-An atom is the smallest particles of element, that can take part in chemical reaction. Atoms are very very small in size.

➤ Molecules:-A molecule is the smallest particles of a substance (element and compound). Which has the property of the substance and can exist in the free state.

☆ Molecules of an Element:-The molecules of an element contain two or more similar atoms chemically combined together.
Ex:- H2, O2, P4,...etc.

☆ Molecules of an Compound:-The molecules of an compound contain two or more different type of atoms chemically combined together.
Ex:- HCl, H2O, H2So4,...etc.

➤ Law of Conservation of Mass:- According to law of conservation of mass: In a chemical reaction the total mass of product is equal to total mass of reactant.

☆There is no change in mass during the chemical reaction.

➤ Law of constant proportions :- According to law of constant proportions: A chemical compound always consist of a same element combined together in the same proportions by mass.

☆ Ex:- Water (H2O) is a compound. ratio of hydrogen and oxygen, one molecules of water is 1:8 by mass.

Note:-The radious of atom indicate the size of atoms. Atomic size is measured in nanometers (nm).(1nm = 10-9m)

➤ Dalton's atomic theory

• All mater is made of very tiny particles called atoms.

• Atoms are indivisible.

• Atoms can not be created and not destroyed in chemical reaction.

• Atoms combine in the ratio of small whole numbers to form compounds.

• Atoms of a given element are identical in mass and chemical properties.

• The relative number and kinds of atoms are constant in a given compound.
